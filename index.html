Our Services

Five Services. One Ecosystem.

Measurable Results.

Every solution we build lives inside your existing Microsoft 365 environment — no new platforms, no new vendors, and no disruption to how your team works today.

01

Power Apps — Custom Business Apps

Replace spreadsheets and paper forms with mobile-ready apps built for your exact processes — fast for SMBs, scalable and governed for enterprise and government.

  • Approval, intake & case management apps
  • Field service, inspection & audit tools
  • Inventory, asset & job tracking systems
  • Staff self-service & citizen portals

02

Power Automate — Workflow Automation

Eliminate repetitive manual tasks at any scale. We design flows that run 24/7 — handling approvals, notifications, data syncing, and cross-system integrations without human input.

  • Multi-stage approvals & compliance flows
  • HR onboarding, offboarding & leave management
  • Cross-system data sync & API integrations
  • Automated reporting, alerts & escalations

03

Power BI — Reports & Dashboards

From a single operational dashboard for a small team to an enterprise-wide data platform — we turn your raw data into real-time decisions every level of your organisation can act on.

  • Executive KPI & performance dashboards
  • Sales, finance & operational reporting
  • Impact & donor reporting for non-profits
  • Enterprise data models & semantic layers

04

Copilot Studio — AI Assistants & Bots

Deploy intelligent virtual assistants that handle queries, process requests, and escalate complex issues — for customers, citizens, staff, or volunteers, inside Teams or on your website.

  • Customer & citizen self-service bots
  • Internal HR, IT & policy helpdesk agents
  • Donor engagement & volunteer onboarding bots
  • Multilingual, accessible AI conversation agents

05

SharePoint Online — Intranet & Document Hub

One organised, searchable, secure hub for all your documents, policies, and team knowledge — built for your structure, whether you have 10 people or 10,000.

  • Company intranets & departmental team sites
  • Document libraries with full version control
  • Policy, compliance & knowledge management
  • Deep integration with Power Apps & Automate
